A Positive Attitude at Work

10 Tips for Success

Tip #1

Report problems quickly and professionally.


the squeaky wheel gets the grease

Tip #2

Treat your coworkers with respect.


You don’t have to like everyone you work with to keep a positive attitude at work.

Tip #3

Get enough sleep.


Go to bed earlier so you get the sleep you need to feel positive.

Tip #4

Identify negative thoughts.


Start to be more aware of your thoughts, words, and actions.

Tip #5

Work on your overall stress level.


If you’re stressed about your marriage or relationship, your kids, your finances, or other problems, it’s really hard to maintain a positive attitude at work.

Tip #6

Set goals.


Nothing can be more soul-crushing that feeling like you’re on a spinning hamster wheel.

Tip #7

Alter your responsibilities.


It never hurts to ask, as long as you do so professionally (see tip number 1).

Tip #8

Smile!


Smile even while you are by yourself.
